Conclusion : Concept of POINT OF REFERENCE

Steps :

  1. We open the monthly chart or the highest time frame chart available.

  2. We make the Wave count and mark the point where a Wave completed on the monthly time frame or we determine a significant low point or high point and we mark it as the Point of Reference.

  3. We now move to 1 lower time frame -

    Monthly to Weekly time frame

  4. Now from the point marked as Point of Reference we start counting the Waves of one lesser degree and the point where the last wave in this count gets completed is marked as the new Point of Reference.

  5. We now shift to further lower time frame -

    Weekly to daily and start counting waves from the new Point of Reference.

  6. By repeating these steps over and over again we can go to time frames as low as 1 min. chart but doing so would be too complicated at this point.

Note – It is advised that you first practice this concept only uptill the 1 Hour time frame and later proceed to even lower time frames, personally I prefer not to go below the 5 minutes time frame unless there is a specific need for the same.
